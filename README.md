# EcoGuard


Once you have generated the dashboard.p12 file, you need to load it into your browser's trust store.

    For Chrome / Edge:

        Go to Settings > Privacy and security > Security.

        Scroll down and click Manage certificates.

        Go to the Your Certificates (or Personal) tab and click Import.

    For Firefox:

        Go to Settings > Privacy & Security.

        Scroll down to the Certificates section and click View Certificates.

        Under the Your Certificates tab, click Import.
